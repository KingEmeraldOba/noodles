NIGERIAN JELLOF RECIPE

Descrption

Jellop Rice is a delicious and fast meal common with many Nigeria families. Jellof rice is just simply cooking the rice and the stew together with a little touch of golden hands that will make it taste differently from the regular rice and stew.

Jellof rice is loved by all children and adults alike. Personnaly, jellof rice is my favourite rice recipe

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • vegetable oil
  • sachet tomatoes, fresh tomatoes and pepper
  • onions, carrot and green peas.
  • bay leaf
  • seasoning cubes and spices
  • Chicken or Beef

Steps:

  1. Per-boil the rice (half done) and sieve
  2. Blend the fresh tomatoes and pepper, dice your onion, carrot and green peas and set aside
  3. Put a clean pot on a low heat and add your vegetable oil once the pot is dry.
  4. when the oil is hot add your onions, then sachet tomatoes and stir. later add your blended tomatoes and pepper. add your chicken water to it
  5. add your per-boiled rice and stir, add more water if the chicken water isnt enough. add your seasoning cubes
  6. monitor so that you can add more wate if needed. Let the water dry, while still on the fire add your carrots, bay leaf and green peas
  7. When the water is completely dried you can serve and add your fried chicken or beef on it
  8. Jellof rice is ready
