Egusi Recipe

Descrption

Eguzi is a Nigeria soup eaten by all Nigerians. It is hard to tell which ethic group egusi soup originated from. Egusi soup is eaten with any swallow of choice.

As a young Nigerian Lady or a young wife married into Nigeria Egusi soup must be amonst the list of Nigeria deliciouse meals you should have as a skill. in this lesson we would see how to prepare the Nigeria Egusi soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of Egusi
  • Palm oil
  • onions
  • dry fish or stock fish
  • Beef or Chicken
  • Ugu leaf
  • pepper
  • Prewincle
  • seasoning cube

Steps:

  1. Slice your ugu leaf, dice your onions, and blend your pepper
  2. cook your Meat, put your egusi in a small bowl and add small water from the meat you just per boiled. and set aside
  3. Put a clean pot on fire, add your palm oil, let it heat up a bit then add your onions and then your egusi
  4. Stir for some time the add more meat water of if you dont have any more add ordinary water. add all your proteins and let the soup boil for about 25mins
  5. add your prewincle and ugu leaf and leave it for 2mins then bring it down. your soup is ready

Egusi is one of Nigerans most simple meals to prepare. your Egusi is ready and you can eat it with any swallow of your choice. Let me know if you tried this recipe.
