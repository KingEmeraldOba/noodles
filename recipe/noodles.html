MY NOODLES RECIPES

Descrption

Hello everyone, how are you all doing? My name is King Emerald and I am a Nigerian who live in Nigeria. I want to share my homemade Nigerian noodles (fried noodles) recipe with you guys. Please Enjoy.

Noodle is a fast food in Nigeria and a meal one can easily prepare when they are hungry. My favourite noodle is the Indomie Noodle. Indomie is a brand of instant noodle produced by the Indonesian company, Indofood.

However, Dufil Prima Foods PLC is the company in Nigeria with the franchise that produces Indomie. Dufil is home to Nigeria's most loved noodle brand, Indomie Instant Noodles. Through consistent backward integration efforts over the past decade, Dufil has established itself as the leader in the instant noodles category. Dufil Group comprises of Dufil Prima Foods Plc.

Ingredients:

  • 3 70g noodles
  • 1 carrot
  • green peas
  • 1 small onion
  • vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs

Steps:

  1. Add three cups of water in a pot amd put on the fire and let the water boil
  2. Add the three noodles in the boiling water and add one seasoning from the noodles to it, keep three seasoning aside
  3. Let it boil for about 5mins, sieve and set aside
  4. Boil your egg and set aside
  5. Dice the carrot, onion and green peas
  6. Add a little vegetable oil(about 2spoons) in a dry pot on low heat,
  7. Add the diced onion, carrot and green peas
  8. Let it heat up a bit. Then add the remaining spices from the noodles, stir and add the noodles.
  9. Stir properly and serve. Add your eggs
